How To Keep You Safe Online

Turn on multifactor authentication

Implement multifactor authentication on all accounts to reduce the chance of getting hacked.

Think before you click

Over 90% of cyber-attacks are successfully initiated by a phishing email. So before you click on any links or popups, slowdown consider who, what and why.

Keep Software Updated

Newer software not only ensures better performance but better security, to make this easier enable automatic updates so you don't have to think about updating.

Use strong Passwords

Strong passwords make it harder for hackers to gain access, Use a password manager they generate and store strong and unique passwords for you so you can't lose or forget
